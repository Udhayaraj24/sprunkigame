Frequently Asked Questions

How do I unlock new characters?
▼
Rescue trapped customers throughout the levels to unlock them as playable characters. Each character has their own unique abilities!
What are the different types of abilities?
▼
Characters have various abilities like double jump, gliding, wall jump, and special attacks. Experiment with different characters to find your favorite playstyle!
How do I beat tough bosses?
▼
Study boss patterns, use your character's abilities wisely, and look for opportunities to attack. Don't forget to collect power-ups before boss battles!
Are there any secret areas?
▼
Yes! Each level contains hidden areas with special collectibles and coins. Look for suspicious walls or platforms that might lead to secret passages!
